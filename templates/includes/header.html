Game: Select a game from the list.

Numbers: Type 1 or more numbers separated by a comma or space to look up combinations of lottery winning numbers.

* Number: Special number such as mega or power number. 1 number only.

Min # of occurrence: Show results that have at least this many occurrences. 10 is default.

Available soon. Displaying the most common numbers and combinations.

LT Combo app analyzes the number of occurrences from combinations of all lottery winning numbers. Occurrences are updated daily based on data from https://data.ny.gov/. This app does neither promote playing the games nor predict future winning numbers, so use it at your own risk.
